Assasin Team Update

Assasin Team Update:
How to play:  In the game of assassin you and your team of three will be assigned another team of three to assassinate. Your mission, if you choose to accept it is to assassinate the members of your target team using any source of water, poison (salt), and time bombs. Each team will designate a team captain, if the captain dies, the team is out. Once you have successfully assassinated your targeted team you will move on to the team they were assigned. Keep in mind that while you are assigned a team to assassinate, another team will be targeting you. NO Freshman. Sophomores must be on a team with two upperclassmen. All players must be affiliated with Berkeley High School. Students attending IS and Btech may play, but will not be eligible for captain. Captains are not allowed to leave the Immediate bay area (NO sf, Shithole suburbs, or any place south of Oakland) or your team will be disqualified.

Honor Code: This is a one of the best Berkeley High traditions, so don't fuck it up. There is an honor code in this game. If strong evidence tells us that someone has broken the honor code by lying about a kill or having someone not on your team plant a bomb, or having a dead person plant bomb/assassinate someone will result in team ejection from the game.
Methods of Assassinating: To kill someone you must wet the back of the neck, hit the person with a water balloon/ball, time bomb, or poison them by adding salt to their drink. 99.99% of the time we won't be there when a kill takes place, so if there's any dispute about a kill, get witnesses or video evidence to confirm it. We can only make decisions about a kill based on what we hear. Remember, the more evidence you have the easier it is for us to confirm kills.

Wet the Neck:  The neck must be clearly wet (the skin), if the target is wearing a hoodie or scarf, etc. this blocks your neck from an attack. The clothing must be removed so that the skin of the neck must be clearly wet to make the kill count.

Balloon/water ball: For a kill to count with a balloon/water ball/sponge, it must hit the body directly, so if it hits a backpack, the kill does not count. The target must be clearly wet and Spray from the explosion of the balloon will not count either. Cups of water and water guns do not count unless they wet the neck.

Time Bombs:  Any device with a timer that makes some sort of noise is considered a time bomb. The alarm must be loud enough for the person to hear. An active member of the team must plant the bomb (if you get killed you cannot plant a bomb.) For a time bomb to be counted as a kill it must be on the target, on
or connected to your target's desk, chair, backpack, pocket, interior of car, or physically on them as long as it goes off while on your targets person. Putting it on the floor by the person doesn't count. It must be on the desk or chair and the target must be sitting at the desk or touching the desk when the bomb goes off. Anyone who plants a time bomb must not be in the room when the bomb goes off or If you are in the room/15 feet away when a timer goes off you are stunned until midnight of that day. If You plant a Timer on the interior of a car you must not be in the car when it goes off or you will be stunned. Timers cannot be placed on shared desks. Phone alarms are allowed.

Poison: To successfully poison your target you must add salt to any liquid that they are drinking. The liquid must clearly taste like salt. the kill counts when the drink enters the targets mouth. Even if they don’t swallow, they are still dead.

Defense: There are ways you can protect yourself other than just being sneaky. You can spray or hit your attacker with water anywhere on the body and they will be stunned (unable to make a kill) until midnight of that day. You can also stun with a water balloon or sponge. If you are stunned you have to tell one of the admins. You can only stun people where water play is normally allowed. You may not stun someone with timers. If you are stunned, you can still stun people,
just not kill.

Safe Zones: Safe zones only apply to water play. Timers and poison are always acceptable. A safe zone is a designated place or event where water play is not allowed. Berkeley High campus is a safe zone. Off campus labs and field trips are safe. Sports practices are safe as well. Home sports games are safe zones, away games are not. The street between the park and school are open to water play, but if both feet are on the curb then they are safe. Cross-walks are not safe. In addition one's work/job's (with a schedule provided to us runners) is a safe zone. The sidewalk surrounding Berkeley high is safe but you must have two feet on the sidewalk to be safe. If you’re in a safe zone but your target isn’t then you may kill them with water.

Trespassing:  You may not enter someone's house without consent under any circumstances. You may not open a gate that is closed, however you can go onto their yard/backyard and porch if the gate is open. You may not climb over or move an obstacle to get into a yard. You may open an unlocked car door. You may not climb onto people’s roofs either.
How to Get Disqualified:  No unnecessary contact. Any pushing, shoving, hitting, tackling or horseplay will result in a kill not counting or ejection from the game. No fucking stealing. NO damaging others property, and lastly, no
TURNING YOUR FUCKING BACK.

Winning : To win the game your team must be the last team standing. The winning team will get a cash prize and have the greatest title at Berkeley High "Assassin King"/"Assassin Queen".
